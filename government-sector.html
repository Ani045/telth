Primary Care

Transforming first-point healthcare

Primary healthcare is the first point of contact for individuals seeking medical services. Efficient, accessible primary care is critical to ensuring the overall health of a population—but in reality, it's often overwhelmed, under-resourced, and disconnected. Long queues, limited diagnostics, and over-reliance on manual processes make early intervention difficult.

Our Solution:

  • AI-powered POCT devices offer real-time diagnostics for 100+ essential parameters in just 3–15 minutes — tailored to primary care needs. View Device ➚
  • Faster, smarter diagnosis AI-driven diagnosis cuts down the process, enabling faster results and allowing doctors to make quicker, more informed decisions.
  • Walk-in AI kiosks placed at the entrance of PHCs reduce the need for multiple visits — patients can self-test, then see the doctor with results in hand. View more ➚
  • No more back-and-forth testing happens upfront, streamlining the care journey and reducing crowding.
  • AI-enabled triage tools guide referrals and care pathways based on test results and symptoms.
  • Integrated with ABHA & G-MED ID for secure, continuous patient history across care levels.
  • Perfect for rural, remote, or high-load primary care centers where speed and simplicity are critical.
  • Empowers ASHA workers and CM/CCMs to initiate diagnostic and triage steps, improving early intervention rates.

Secondary & Tertiary Care

Transforming advanced healthcare delivery

Secondary and tertiary care centers often face overwhelming patient loads, delayed referrals, and poor coordination between departments. Patients wait too long for specialist appointments, and doctors frequently lack access to complete medical histories during critical moments. Manual workflows missed follow-ups, and inefficient triage slow down diagnosis and treatment—leading to repeat visits, overcrowded hospitals, and compromised outcomes.

Our Solution:

  • Root Cloud™ – All patient data in one place, updated in real-time, so nothing gets missed. View More ➚
  • Fast Diagnostic Devices – Run 200+ tests per hour to speed up care in labs, ICUs, and OPDs.
  • HES at Point of Care – Get results for 100+ tests in minutes, right at the point of care—ideal for ambulances, ICUs, and operating rooms when every second matters.
  • Telth Hubs – Our comprehensive "Telth Hub" stations are expanded kiosks packed with integrated diagnostics, tele-consults, and remote monitoring capabilities for rapid specialist access, second opinions, and structured follow-ups.
  • G-MED ID + ABHA – Patient records that travel with them, connected and secure.
  • AI-Powered Support – Smart suggestions for doctors based on patient vitals and history—instantly.

Frontline Health Workers (ASHAs)

Empowering Healthcare's Frontline Heroes

Frontline health workers, like ASHAs, are the backbone of rural and semi-urban healthcare, but they often face challenges such as lack of proper tools, limited resources, and inconsistent work schedules. In many areas, ASHAs manage multiple duties without the necessary support, leading to data gaps, delayed interventions, and inefficient health monitoring. Their work, though critical, can be fragmented, especially in regions with fewer workers or lower technology access.

Our Solution:

  • Smart mobile devices preloaded with data collection and screening tools to support health checks like diabetes and glucose monitoring.
  • Remote consultation access allows ASHAs to connect with healthcare providers in real time for advice and guidance.
  • Multi-language training modules empowering ASHAs with continuous, easy-to-understand education.
  • Integration of HES (Health Examination System) devices for on-the-spot diagnostics in remote areas, ensuring conditions are caught early.
  • CM/CCM support network to fill staffing gaps, ensuring frontline workers are available for all necessary interventions.View more ➚
